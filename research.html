Research

Journal Articles

  • Age at School Entry and Human Capital Development
    American Economic Journal: Applied Economics (Forthcoming)
    with Jacob Bor & Jan-Walter De Neve

    Studies the causal effect of school-entry age on educational and human capital outcomes in Lesotho using a regression discontinuity design.

    Forthcoming · AEJ:Applied
  • The Causal Effect of Girls' Education on Cancer Awareness and Screening
    Nature Communications, 2025
    with Janny Liao, Jan-Walter De Neve, Michelle Holmes & S. V. Subramanian

    Uses a natural experiment in Lesotho to estimate the causal effect of additional education on women's cancer awareness and screening behaviour.

    Published · Nature Communications
  • The Effect of Weather Shocks on Women's Labour Supply
    Journal of African Development, 2025
    with Mamello Nchake & Luca Tiberti

    Estimates income effects of weather shocks on labour supply of women in women-headed households in Lesotho.

    Published · Journal of African Development
  • School-Entry Age and Health Outcomes: A Scoping Review
    SSM — Population Health, 2023
    with Kerim Munir and others

    Scoping review examining the evidence on school-entry age and health outcomes, identifying research gaps in low- and middle-income countries.

    Published · SSM Population Health
  • Long-Term Effects of Primary Education Expansion in Lesotho
    Annals of Economics and Statistics, 2023

    Examines the long-term impact of Lesotho's primary education expansion on human capital and labour market outcomes.

    Published · Annals of Economics & Statistics
  • Changes in Educational Achievement and Inequality in Lesotho
    Studies in Economics and Econometrics, 2022

    Uses relative distribution analysis to study trends in educational achievement and inequality over time in Lesotho.

    Published · Studies in Economics and Econometrics
  • Market Structure and Bank Conduct in Lesotho
